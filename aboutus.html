Our Company

Our company is established in 1999, called "XinYue Shoes" and changed the name to "AoQiao Shoes" since 2014. Complying "people-oriented, technology first, quality leading, customer paramount, developing together, sharing equitable" principle. We have got a rapid progress and have been a product design, insole production, backing putting and shoes finishing modern enterprise.

We mainly produce sport shoes, hiking shoes, casual shoes, sandals, flyknits shoes and waterproof shoes, with about 100,000 pairs per month production capacity.

There are more than 200 employee working under comfortable working environment and being full of happiness everyday.

We provide OEM service. And we have full confidence that we can bring you high quality, competitive price and considerate service. Welcome to contact us anytime if you have any request and look forward to working with you to build mutual-beneficial business in future.
